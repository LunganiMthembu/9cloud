Our Story

A Legacy Brewed Through Time

Founded in 1892, Café Lore has been serving exceptional coffee for over a century. Our journey began with a simple mission: to bring the finest coffee experiences to those who appreciate the art of brewing.

From our humble beginnings as a small roastery, we've grown into a beloved institution while maintaining our commitment to quality, tradition, and the perfect cup.

Every bean tells a story, every cup holds history, and every sip connects you to generations of coffee artisans who have perfected their craft.
